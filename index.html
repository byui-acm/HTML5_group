		RULES
1) You have 4 actions per turn.
2) Movements, attacks, and spells all consume 1 action.
3) Units can move in blue lit ranges.
4) Units can attack in red lit ranges.
5) Wizards flip tiles in green range to opposite board.
6) Flipped tiles transfer units with them.
7) Kill all enemy units.
